Our Purpose

A student's background
should never determine
their access to opportunity.

Across Aotearoa, there are capable, curious, and motivated students who are ready to step beyond their immediate environment. Yet too often, financial barriers stand in the way. Talent is everywhere — but opportunity is not.

Te Ara Hihiko Trust creates meaningful pathways for students who may otherwise never access international learning. These aren't luxuries. They are transformative experiences that shape how young people see themselves and their place in the world.

A student's story

Before this trip, I had never travelled outside of New Zealand.

"When I first arrived, everything felt unfamiliar. But as the days went by, I began to try new things, speak up more, and connect with people in ways I never thought I could. I came back more independent, more open-minded, and more motivated to achieve my goals."
S
Year 11 Student South Auckland · 2025 Cohort

At the start, I didn't want to speak at all. By the end of the programme, I was presenting in front of the whole group.
